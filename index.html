About

Dentistry with
depth & dedication

Mrs Olesya Pavlova is a GDC-registered dentist with a performer number, practising across Surrey in fully private and mixed NHS/private settings. She combines genuine clinical skill with warm, patient-centred care — taking time to understand each patient's goals and building personalised treatment plans that put long-term oral health first.

Her clinical background spans general and restorative dentistry, endodontics, and facial aesthetics. She is proficient with the latest dental technologies: rotary endodontic systems, dental loupes, intraoral cameras, digital radiography, and dental microscopes — tools that allow for greater precision and less invasive treatment.

Olesya is currently pursuing a Master's in Restorative Dentistry with Tipton Training, reflecting her commitment to staying current in the profession and offering patients thoughtful, up-to-date care.

Expertise

Areas of clinical
specialisation

Fillings, Crowns & Repairs

From simple fillings to crowns, bridges, and dentures — restoring damaged or missing teeth as carefully and conservatively as possible.

Root Canal Treatment

Saving a tooth that's badly decayed or infected, rather than removing it. Carried out with precision tools and magnification to keep things as comfortable as possible.

Facial Aesthetics

Non-surgical treatments such as anti-wrinkle injections and dermal fillers — a natural complement to dental care for patients who want to feel their best.

Modern Technology

Using digital X-rays, scanning tools, and magnification equipment to see more clearly, diagnose accurately, and make visits as straightforward as possible.

Gum Health

Caring for your gums with practical advice, cleaning, and treatment for gum disease — because healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy mouth.

Personal Treatment Plans

Taking the time to understand what you want from your dental care and putting together a clear, honest plan that works around your needs and lifestyle.
